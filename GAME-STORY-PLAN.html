The Dark Road

In the near future, a secret organization called DARK ROAD controls illegal weapons, AI warfare, and global conflicts. You play as RIDER-01, an elite spy sent to dismantle the system before humanity collapses.

LEVEL 1 — City Shadows

Night falls over a corrupted city. Alone in the shadows, you infiltrate enemy zones using stealth, hacking puzzles, and silent sniper kills.

  • Spy & stealth gameplay
  • Puzzle hacking systems
  • Rooftop sniper missions
  • Escape through city chases

LEVEL 2 — Highway Deathrun

A high-speed chase turns into survival horror when a bio-weapon leaks. Enemies and zombies flood the highways.

  • Vehicle combat (cars & bikes)
  • Highway gunfights
  • Zombie survival waves
  • Convoy destruction

LEVEL 3 — Desert War Base

Stealth is over. This is full-scale war. You assault a massive desert military base protected by armies and war machines.

  • FPS / TPS switching combat
  • Enemy armies & commanders
  • Tanks, helicopters & explosions
  • Arena-style boss battle

LEVEL 4 — Mech Fortress

Deep underground lies the Mech Fortress—home of AI-controlled robots and war mechs. Every mistake is punished.

  • Advanced action combat
  • Souls-like difficulty
  • Robots & giant mechs
  • Special anti-mech vehicles

LEVEL 5 — Dark Road Final War

The final war erupts across cities, skies, and bases. All action types collide in a world-shaking battle.

  • Spy, shooting, vehicles & armies
  • Mechs and elite enemies
  • 2 Transformers-level final fight
  • Multiple endings
